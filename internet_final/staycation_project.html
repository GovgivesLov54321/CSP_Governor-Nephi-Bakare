Capitol Reef Staycation Adventure

Capitol Reef National Park is a hidden gem in the heart of southern Utah. With its towering sandstone cliffs, ancient petroglyphs, and vast scenic drives, it's a perfect place to escape and recharge. Unlike more crowded parks, Capitol Reef offers serenity and unspoiled beauty ideal for a peaceful staycation.

Top Reasons to Visit Capitol Reef

  • Stunning red rock landscapes and rock formations
  • Explore historic Fruita orchards and scenic drives
  • Hiking trails for all skill levels
  • Stargazing under one of the darkest skies in the U.S.

Capitol Reef is known for its Waterpocket Fold, a 100-mile wrinkle in the Earth's crust. The park is also home to fascinating geology, wildlife, and the charming Fruita district where visitors can pick seasonal fruit. It’s the perfect destination for nature lovers, photographers, and families alike.
